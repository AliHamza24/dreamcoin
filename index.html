What is Dream?

DreamCoin is a frictionless, high-yield, auto deflationary crypto
much like bitcoin on the Binance Smart Chain. A 14%
transaction fee rewards holders and another mechanism will
burn 40% of the contract. This is a token that could
potentially 10x, 100x and even 1000x in the near future with
the most advanced tokenomics created.

Renewable Energy

Like bitcoin & other cryptocurrencies, long-term renewable
tokenomics is mirrored in one Dream's core value. Building
on this, the leaders of the protocol aim to execute renewable
vision through new ideas & help from the community. Our
marketing powers our community & our community powers
our marketing.

Binance Smart Chain

Dream is powered by the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). This
blockchain network runs in parallel to the Binance Chain. The
speed and low fees of this specific blockchain offers our
community the added interoperability and affordability of
facilitating investments via MetaMask and other wallets.
